Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions we hear most often. Can't find yours? Send us a message above.

What is SecureAI?

SecureAI is an AI-powered cybersecurity platform that detects, prevents, and responds to cyber threats in real time. We use machine learning to analyse network behaviour, identify anomalies, and protect your data, networks, and users from modern attacks.

Is SecureAI suitable for individuals or only businesses?

SecureAI is designed for both. Individuals benefit from our educational content and threat awareness tools, while businesses access advanced AI monitoring, threat detection, and incident response capabilities tailored to enterprise needs.

How quickly does SecureAI detect threats?

Our AI engine analyses network traffic and user behaviour continuously, detecting anomalies in milliseconds. Most known threats are blocked instantly, while novel patterns are flagged and contained within seconds.

Do I need technical knowledge to use SecureAI?

Not at all. SecureAI is built with a simple dashboard and clear alerts so anyone can use it. For deeper customisation, we provide documentation, tutorials, and a dedicated support team.

What types of cyber threats does SecureAI cover?

We protect against phishing, malware, ransomware, deepfakes, prompt injection, fraud, unauthorised access, and emerging AI-driven attacks. Our threat database is updated continuously with the latest intelligence.

How do I report a security vulnerability?

Email security@secureai.com with details. We follow responsible disclosure practices and acknowledge all reports within 24 hours. Critical vulnerabilities are eligible for our bug bounty programme.
