AI vs Cybercrime

AI in cybersecurity is used to protect systems and detect threats, while cybercrime involves malicious activities aimed at attacking systems and stealing data.

As cybercriminals become more sophisticated, AI is stepping in as a powerful defence tool. This page explores how AI is being used to fight back against cybercrime, and how criminals are also attempting to misuse AI for malicious purposes.

Understanding both sides helps individuals and organisations stay informed, recognise risks, and take proactive steps to improve cybersecurity in an evolving digital world.

How AI Fight Cybercrime

Fraud Detection

Spots and blocks suspicious activity instantly.

Phishing Prevention

Detects and filters scam emails automatically.

How Criminals Misuse AI

Deepfakes

Fake videos used to impersonate people.

AI Phishing

Auto-generates convincing scam messages.
